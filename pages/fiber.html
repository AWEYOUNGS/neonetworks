Fiber Optics Link Implementation and Planning

You can count on our installation.Planning and design solutions team to take care of all your Fiber Optics cabling requirements. All of our solutions in this area are within your budget and requirements needs in mind. We offer a very cost effective cabling solution with your IT budget in mind, while still maintaining quality and integrity.

Our Fiber Optics Link Implementation and Planning Includes:

  1. TSS ( Technical Site Survey) and Technical Site Drawing
  2. Detailed ROW Acquisition
  3. PVC Pipe Laying
  4. Man Holes and Hand Holes construction
  5. Thrust-boring and Asphalt cutting
  6. Fiber Optics Cable Blowing, Pulling (Manual),Digging and Back-filling
  7. Fiber Optics Drum Testing
  8. Fiber Optics Splicing, Termination and Testing
  9. Fiber Optics Loss Budget Planning
  10. As-built Prep using Auto-cad
  11. Fiber Optics route B.E.R,Attenuation,Chromatic Dispersion and P.M.D testing
